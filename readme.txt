Background Slideshow is a nice jQuery-powered Slideshow, that uses unlimited full-window-sized images to offer a nice effect to your visitors.

== Description ==

Background Slideshow uses jQuery to slide through unlimited full-size background images. Images are being connected with both, posts or pages. You can even decide if you want to let your visitors click on a background image to change to its post/page. Background Slideshow offers some nice features for example:

* jQuery Powered lightweight slideshow
* Nice flewing full-size background images
* Link to post/pages
* Full post-thumbnail support
* Add unlimited images
* Use on your whole blog or only on specific pages/posts
* Choose timeout/animation speed

Check out the <a href="http://preview.wp-themix.org/plugins/background-slideshow/" target="_blank">DEMO</a> at <a href="http://www.iwebix.de" target="_blank" title="webdesign">IWEBIX</a>

== Installation ==

1. Upload Background Slideshow to your Plugin directory (wp-content/plugins/).
2. Activate the Plugin.
3. Go to Settings/Background Slideshow and customize the Slideshow to your needs.
3. Use placeholder [background] or choose "use on whole blog" within the Settings Page.
4. Edit/add a post or page and choose "Feature in Background Slideshow".
5. Add a featured image (Should be a full-size image).
6. Finished - See the results!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I insert the Background Slideshow? =

Insert it anywhere on every post/page you want with the placeholder [background] or choose "use on whole blog" within the Settings Page.

= What is perfect Resolution for the Images? =

Your resolution should be at least 1024 px width. The bigger the better to fit all desktop resolutions.
